About Me

Hi, I'm Asher. I'm currently a sophomore at Westview High School. Having spent about seven or eight years living in Vienna, I am fluent in both English and German.

Music and sports are a major part of my everyday life. I've been playing piano for 11 years—including seven years of formal classical training—and I currently run a business teaching one-on-one piano lessons to kids. When I'm not composing my own remixes and arrangements, you can usually find me on the field as a JV soccer player, lifting weights at the gym, or working on my e-bike.

Looking ahead to university, I'm focused on combining my interests by studying international business and music.
